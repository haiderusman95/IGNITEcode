Digital Pakistan Speed Programming Competition (codePK)

A team-based programming competition that fosters problem-solving, programming and teamwork skills.

Objectives

  • Skill Development
  • Problem Solving
  • Community Building
  • Healthy Competition
  • Time Management & Pressure Handling
  • Promoting Innovation
  • Identifying Talent
  • Exposure to New Technologies
  • Global Representation

Languages Supported

Java, C/C++/.Net, Python

Format

Online preliminary round followed by an on-site competition for qualifiers.

How to Participate

Free registration open till 30th May 2025 for Computer Science/Engineering students.

Winning Criteria

  • Highest Score
  • Quickest Submission
  • Plagiarism-Free Content

Awards

Winner: Rs.1,500,000
1st Runner-up: Rs.1,000,000
2nd Runner-up: Rs.500,000
